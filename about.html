Game of Luck
About

Game of Luck is a game with the concept of rock-paper-scissor game. It has 3 protagonist that represents the rock, paper, and scissor, they are: Roccian(rock), Palma(paper), and Esclicer(scissor). The antagonist are some gladiators that also posses the same power as the main characters. It depends on the luck of the character selected if he/she will face a gladiator with a power that is useless against him/her.
